2. Information We Collect or Process / ข้อมูลที่เราเก็บหรือประมวลผล

We may collect or process the following information depending on how you use the app:

  • Account information: Firebase user ID used by the app. If you connect Google Sign-In, we may process your Google display name, email address, and profile photo URL for sign-in and account display. TellDream does not store or show your Google account ID.
  • Legal consent records: privacy policy version, terms version, acceptance time, app version, platform, and selected sign-in method.
  • Dream input: text you type and text produced by voice-to-text when you submit it for interpretation. We process this input to generate interpretations and to validate that the text complies with our content safety guidelines (requiring appropriate and polite language).
  • Voice input: the app uses Android or Google speech services available on your device to convert speech to text. TellDream does not store raw voice audio.
  • AI request and response data: submitted dream text, app-provided instruction text needed to request the interpretation, AI outputs/completions, keywords, two-digit numbers, three-digit numbers, disclaimer, model metadata, prompt version, and processing status.
  • AI results and history: interpretation, keywords, number suggestions, disclaimer, creation date, saved history, and deletion status.
  • Dream reference usage: the ตำราฝัน feature uses reference data bundled with the app so it can work offline. Search text in this feature does not need to be sent to our server to show reference results.
  • Aggregate trend data: AI keywords and numbers may be normalized and counted in aggregate to create ฝันยอดฮิต. This feature does not show names, UID, dream text, or another person's history.
  • Quota and ad reward data: remaining interpretation credits, rewarded ad credit events, reward transaction IDs, AdMob server-side verification metadata where available, and related audit records.
  • Advertising data: banner ad requests, rewarded ad requests, impressions, ad interactions, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and device signals processed by Google AdMob according to Google policies and your device settings.
  • Analytics and diagnostics: app events, feature usage, app version, device model, operating system version, crash reports, performance data, and similar diagnostic information.
  • Security and integrity data: Firebase Authentication tokens during API requests, Firebase App Check or Play Integrity signals, Cloudflare request metadata, IP-derived request information, rate-limit records, and abuse-prevention logs.
  • Support communications: information you send when contacting us, such as email address, message, screenshots, or device details needed to help resolve your request.
